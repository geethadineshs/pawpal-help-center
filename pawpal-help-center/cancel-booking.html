Cancel Booking

After successfully creating a booking, you can view all your bookings.

To cancel a specific booking:

1.Click on the "Settings" menu.

2.Select the "My Bookings" tab on the Settings page

3.You will be directed to the "My Bookings" page, where you can view all your bookings. Additionally, there will be filters available for you to easily access bookings based on specific dates and time slots.

4.You can cancel only the bookings with "Approved" and "Pending" status.

5.Click the "X" icon on that booking, and a confirmation box will appear

6.Confirm the cancellation process by selecting "Confirm" in the confirmation box.

7.Your booking is now cancelled, and the status will be updated as “Cancelled”.

8.Clicking the "Cancel" button in the Confirmation box redirects you to the “My Bookings” page without cancelling the booking.

Please note that you will be charged according to the cancellation policy.
