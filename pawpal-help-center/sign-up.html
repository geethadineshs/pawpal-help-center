Sign Up

Find the app:

Locate the paw pal app icon on your device's home screen

Android

IOS

Open the app:

Tap on the paw pal app icon to open it.

New user:

If you're a new user, sign up first to proceed.

Sign Up:

1. As a new user, you need to create an account.

2. Below the 'Continue' button, you can find "Don't have an account? Sign Up."

3. Tap 'Sign Up' to start the account creation process.

Choose How to Sign Up:

To sign up, you have three options:

  • Continue with Google.
  • Continue with Facebook.
  • Continue with email.

Sign Up with Google:

1.To sign up using Google, tap the 'Continue with Google' button.

2.Select the Google account

3.You’ll be directed to “Add your info” page

Sign up with email:

To sign up using email:

1.Enter your email.

2.Click on the 'Continue' button. You will be prompted to verify your email with a One-Time Password.

3. Enter the password

(or)

4. Copy the password and go back to the app and tap the 'Paste' button to paste the password.

5.Click on the 'Sign up' button. You are successfully signed up and redirected to the "Add your info" page.

Password Not Received:

If you do not receive the password, click on the "Resend OTP" button.

Add your Info.

1.After completing the “sign up” process, you'll be taken to the 'Add Your Info' page.

2.On the 'Add Your Info' page

3. You can provide your basic information. Please note that your email address cannot be changed.

4.Complete the required details now, and you can add any optional information later.

5.Click the “Continue” button to proceed to add information about your pet

Add pet:

1.After adding your information, you'll be taken to the 'Add Pet' page.

2.On the 'Add Pet' page:

3.Provide your pet's name, date of birth and select your pet's gender, pet's size, pet's breed.

4.Fill out the above required details. Additional optional information can be filled in later.

5.And click “Save” button

6.You'll land on a page.

This page stays visible until your user account gets approved.

Sign Up with Facebook:

1.To sign up using Facebook, tap the 'Continue with Facebook' button.

2.Select the Facebook account and click the "Continue" button.

3.You’ll be directed to “Add your info” page
