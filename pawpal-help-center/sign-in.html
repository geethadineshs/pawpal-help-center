User Sign-In Handbook

Choose How to Sign In:

To sign in, you have three options:

  • Continue with Google
  • Continue with Facebook
  • Continue with email

Sign In with Google:

Please select the Google account you used to sign up.

1. To sign in using Google, tap the "Continue with Google" button.

2. Select the Google account you used during the sign-up process.

3. You'll be directed to "Request Booking" page

Sign In with email:

To sign in using email:

1. Enter the email address you used during the sign-up process.

2. Click on the 'Continue' button. You will be prompted to verify your email with a One-Time Password.

3. Enter the password.

(or)

4. Copy the password and go back to the app and tap 'Paste' button to paste the password.

5. Click on the 'Sign In' button. You are successfully signed in and directed to the "Request Booking" page.

Password Not Received:

If you do not receive the password, click on the "Resend OTP" button.

Sign In with Facebook:

1. To sign in using Facebook, tap the 'Continue with Facebook' button.

2. Select the Facebook account you used during the sign-up process and click the "Continue" button.

3. You’ll be directed to “Request Booking” page.
