Calendar Booking

After successfully logging in, you will be directed to the home screen. Please proceed to make bookings.

Calendar booking with Multiple Pet

1.Your first pet is pre-selected by default, and you have the option to choose an additional pet if you want to book a service for both pets.

2.Tap on the service to choose it for your selected pets

3.The service list will be filtered based on your selected pet.

4.Look for the "Calendar" tab and click on it

5.After clicking on the "Calendar" option, you will see the available time slots and a calendar.

6.Scroll to select the slot

7.Tap on a single date or select multiple dates within the calendar.

8.Click the "Request a Booking" button and you will see a confirmation box.

9.Confirm your booking by clicking "Confirm" in the Confirmation box.

10.Clicking the "Cancel" button in the Confirmation box redirects you to the booking page without creating the booking.
