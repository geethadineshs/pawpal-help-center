Multiple booking with a single Pet

Request Booking:

After successfully logging in, you will be directed to the home screen. Please proceed to make bookings.

To Make Multiple booking with a single Pet:

  1. Your pet has already been chosen. However, if you have multiple pets and you want to change the selected one, you can do so by unselecting the current choice and tapping on another pet.

  1. Next, select a service for your pet.

  2. The service list will be filtered based on your selected pet.

  3. Tap on a service to confirm it for your selected pet. By choosing the service, you will see the available dates for that service.

  1. Scroll to select the date in the date-selection section.

  1. Scroll to select the slot in the slot-selection section.

  1. For Multiple booking Click the "Add Day" button.

  1. Scroll to select the date in the date-selection section.

  1. Scroll to select the slot in the slot-selection section.

  1. Click on the 'X' icon to deselect a specific date and time slot.

  1. If you want to provide more details about your pet, please add them in the 'Notes' section.

  1. Click the "Request a Booking" button, and a confirmation box will appear.

  1. Confirm your booking by clicking "Confirm" in the confirmation box. You'll get an email notification once your booking is confirmed.

  1. Clicking the "Cancel" button in the Confirmation box redirects you to the booking page without creating the booking..
